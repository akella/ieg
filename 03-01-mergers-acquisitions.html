Mergers & Acquisitions

We advise our clients on the acquisition and disposal of companies, stakes in companies, subsidiaries and other assets. In addition, we provide independent valuations and support our clients in the context of mergers, joint ventures and co-operations.

Our support usually starts with the analysis and assessment of options. This leads to clear recommendations, which allow for an optimal decision on the basis of appropriate quantitative and qualitative parameters.

During the execution of transactions, IEG structures and coordinates the overall process and supports its client throughout all the steps up to the successful closing.

The scope of our advisory services in addition to ‚classic‘ M&A advisory also involves the support on capital market transactions (“public tender offers”), succession issues in the context of family-owned businesses, the privatisation of companies in public ownership and the provision of fairness opinions.

Through its international network IEG supports its clients with the identification of potential acquisition opportunities or partners also outside of Germany and particularly in growth markets. Thereby, the structure of IEG group ensures that we can provide optimal solutions to our clients also for smaller transactions.
