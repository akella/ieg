Financial Strategy

The need for corporate finance expertise often arises independently from specific transactions. Depending on the individual issue and situation of our client, we provide specific know-how of IEG in a very focused way.

Our scope of services in the field of financial strategy comprises a broad range of topics that mainly result from the specific requirements and individual needs of our clients – the following list gives an exemplary overview of the kind of projects, that IEG supports on a regular basis:

„Capital structure & financing strategy“: Analysis of companies in regards to the appropriate capital structure and recommendations on optimal financing strategies, e.g. in preparation for expected medium-term financing requirements and for the assessment of the status quo.

„IEG-GLOBUS-Simulation“: Development and implementation of a modular financial planning tool for corporate management based on a detailed and flexible corporate model.

„IEG-PHOENIX-Restructuring“: Modeling of alternative strategies in the context of restructuring situations – especially in regards to different financing options.

Other subject areas are assistance in the implementation of a capital market infrastructure and the development of a corporate governance system as well as the communication with equity investors and lenders.

Part of our financial strategy services are tailored especially towards our medium-sized clients. We enable our clients to selectively and focused add to their team IEG’s long-standing experience in corporate finance. By doing this, we free up resources of the client and enable access to an expertise, which in this scope could only be replicated by maintaining a specialist corporate finance division. For many of our medium-sized clients we offer this kind of expertise permanent on call in the form of long-term mandates.
